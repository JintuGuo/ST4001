Course Drop Request
Before you make a decision to drop a course, please understand that you are not
encouraged to drop any required courses that will affect your progress towards
graduation. In addition, you should follow the instructions below to drop a course
that you enrolled already.
ZJUI Academic Affairs
1. During the first week of the semester, you can drop a course by yourself
through PeopleSoft.
2. If later than the first week, you must submit a request here by the end of 7th
week of instruction in a semester. For Fall 2025, the deadline of drop course is
5PM, Nov 7, 2025. There will be no W (Withdraw) recording on your
transcript if your request is approved. If the course follows UIUC calendar (the
course starts on August 25), the deadline is Oct 17 2025 (UIUC time).
3. If the course you want to drop is UIUC credit (click here to check, UIUC credit
courses are highlighted in yellow), you must submit an additional request
through UIUC COE portal with your UUC NetID by the above deadline. For
the students who pursue ZJU single degree, you do not need to submit
request to UIUC portal.
4. Please don't submit your request on last minute. You will be not be allowed
to drop course after the deadline if nothing happens beyond your control.
5. For some 100- and 200-level courses, you need to go through the portal to
drop the course if you accidently add the course and cannot drop it by self-
service in PS system.
