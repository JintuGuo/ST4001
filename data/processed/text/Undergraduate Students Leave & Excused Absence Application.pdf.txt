Undergraduate Students Leave & Excused
Absence Application
Dear students,
Before submitting your leave of absence application, please carefully read and
understand the following instructions.
 
1. You are not allowed to take a leave for more than four weeks. If this is
the case, you MUST apply to gap the semester.
2. If You apply for leave shorter than 4 weeks and longer than 14 days,
you MUST seek approval from ZJUI Dean, Professor Hao MA in
advance.
3. Unless you are applying for medical leave or family emergency, the
application MUST be submitted at least THREE business days in
advance.
4. In the occasion of emergency for medical leave, you can apply after
your visit to the clinics.
5. For family emergency, you should inform class tutor before you leave
and apply for leave as soon as you come back to campus, if you
cannot apply before you leave the campus.
6. Filling out this online form does NOT guarantee that your application
will be approved and you will have an excused absence.
