ZJUI Course Offerings in Spring Semester 2026
All the courses offered in Spring Semester 2026 are categorized into the following
three types: 
 
Courses are also categorized as mandatory (section 3 in your curriculum) and
elective ones (section 4 in your curriculum). 
 
Please read all the following notes before dive into the course table.
1. UIUC courses are those taught by UIUC faculties or co-taught by ZJUI and
UIUC faculties. The academic credits (AU) earned from UIUC courses  are
therefore counted as UIUC credits. Final grades will be recorded in both ZJUI
and UIUC systems.
All UIUC courses are highlighted in blue in the course tables.
2. ZJUI courses  are those taught by ZJUI faculties normally without the
involvement of UIUC faculties. AUs earned from ZJUI courses are therefore
counted as ZJUI credits.   Grades of these courses will be recorded in
INTL PeopleSoft system and will be transferred to UIUC after students have
been  successfully admitted by UIUC through international transfer during
their junior year.
3. ZJU courses  are those taught by  ZJU faculties that are used to fulfil the
graduation requirement for the ZJU degree.
1. Mandatory courses include fundamental mathematics and science, technical
cores, and composition. Everyone must complete all mandatory courses.
2. Elective courses  include technical courses, general education (liberal
education) and free electives. Elective courses are optional. Students must
take sufficient AUs from elective courses to fulfil the total credit requirement
of both ZJU and UIUC degrees. Free electives can be almost any course under
the categories of ZJUI and UIUC courses.
