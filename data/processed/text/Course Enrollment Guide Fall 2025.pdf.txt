2. All of the override requests should be submitted along with a compelling

Examples of valid justifications include, but not limit to: 
A. the need for an additional course to satisfy graduation requirements;
B. the necessity to repeat a course that will not be offered next semester. 
3. The request is subject to approval by UA Office. Upon submission of a

delivered to you via email later.
4. Application portal for course overrides will open during the two periods of
course registration.
1. Students can drop courses prior to the middle of the semester without “W”
label, as long as total semester credits are no fewer than 12. 
2. Students can drop the course via self-service in PS before 5 pm Sep. 19th,
2025, except for the pre-registered courses by UA office.
3. After the first week of instruction, students must submit an online request to
drop a course. 
A. The application portal will open from 8 am Sep. 20th, 2025. 
B. The request is subject to the approval by the UA Office. 
C. The request should be submitted no later than 5 pm Nov. 7th, 2025 to
avoid a W (Withdraw) label being recorded on the transcript. Please be
aware that dropping courses will NOT be allowed after 5 pm Nov. 7th,
2025 unless there are circumstances beyond the students' control.
4. Students are not allowed to drop pre-registered courses of freshmen and
sophomore courses. Students should go through online portal for course
drop.
5. Students should simultaneously submit a course drop application to
banner system on UIUC side if the course is a UIUC credit course.
6. Students cannot add the courses that follow UIUC calendar after Sept 8th,
2025.

week of instruction in every semester.
2. It is strongly recommended that students establish a personal degree
audit spreadsheet and monitor key metrics such as GPA, credits earned,
etc. in PeopleSoft and UIUC myIllini at least once a semester.
