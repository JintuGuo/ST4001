Zhejiang University 
Automation Curriculum 
(Updated in April 2023) 
 
1. Program Educational Objective 
This program is an ideal choice for those who wish to pursue studies in Industrial Automation, 
System Control, and Robotics. It addresses latest technologies and their applications in control 
science and engineering, enabling students to explore new technology innovations to serve 
social demands.  
 
2. Graduation Requirement 
1) 
Curriculum Requirement 
The curriculum leading to the degree of Bachelor of Science in Automation from ZJU requires 
135 hours and is organized into required courses, technical electives, liberal education, 
Chinese language and society study and other electives.
