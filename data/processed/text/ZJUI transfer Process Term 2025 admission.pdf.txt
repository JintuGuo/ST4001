• Choosing score recipients before the test
o Your test fee includes up to four free official TOEFL score reports to be sent to the 
institutions, agencies or other recipients that you select before you take the test. You can 
add or delete recipients through your ETS account until 10 p.m. (local test center time) 
on the day before your test.
• When institutions will receive your scores
o Score delivery time varies based on how you took the test and how your designated 
score recipients receive scores (all times approximate):
o ETS® Data Manager: 4–8 business days (11–13 business days for Paper Edition)
o Server feeds: 8–10 business days (11–16 business days for Paper Edition)

Important Guidelines
Do not share the application URL or your NetID/password with anyone. This 
link is unique to participants of the ZJU cohort and only can be accessed with 
your NetID and password.
Do not order a University of Illinois transcript.
Do not update your name and birthday within the application unless it is 
incorrect. If incorrect, please consult with the program advisors. There is no 
prefix and suffix in your names. 
If your TOEFL/IELTS/Duolingo score has expired beyond the two-year validity 
mark, you will then need to take a new test and meet the submission deadline.
Turn in application by the deadline, Feb, 15th, 2025. There are no extensions!
